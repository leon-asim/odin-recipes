Pumpkin Pie

Description

This is my personal favourite

Pumpkin pie is on the of my pie rankings. Especially this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin puree(mashed pumpkin)
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Gather the pumpkin puree, eggs, sugar and milk.
  2. Mix pumpkin puree with eggs, sugar and milk until smooth.
  3. Pour the pumpkin mixture into the baking plate.
  4. Place in the oven and bake until the filling sets.
  5. Let the pie cool before slicing and serving.
  6. And lastly enjoy :]
