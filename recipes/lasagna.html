Lasagna

Description

Make the tastiest lasagna at home.

Follow this old italian recipe from my grandma to make your home feel just like Italy.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Gather the lasagna sheets, meat, tomato sauce, and Mozzarella.
  2. Saut√© the meat and mix with tomato sauce.
  3. Alternate layers of sheets, sauce, filling, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Place in the oven and cook until the pasta is tender and the top is golden.
  5. Let it cool slightly before cutting and serving.
  6. And lastly enjoy :]
