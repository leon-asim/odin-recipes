Caesar Salad

Description

It's sharing the name with one of the biggest Roman emperors even though it's not named after him.

This salad is full with nutrients, especially with protein.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan
  • Chicken steak
  • Tomatoes
  • Dressing(egg yolk, mustard, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil)

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Rinse the lettuce and chop it into bite-sized pieces and rinse it in the bowl.
  3. Cut the steak in small pieces and place in the bowl.
  4. Sprinkle croutons and grated parmesan.
  5. Pour Caesar dressing on top, toss gently, and serve.
  6. And lastly enjoy :]
